Pre-Program Prep

Unlock your T-Spine for the Barbell

The 6-Week Foundations involves foundational lifting. If your upper back is tight, squats and presses will feel clunky. Start doing this tonight:

1

The Bench Prayer Stretch

Kneel in front of a chair or bench. Place elbows on the edge, hands together behind your head. Sink your head between your arms and push your chest toward the floor.

2

The Exhale

Hold for 3 deep breaths. On every exhale, try to sink 1cm deeper. This opens up the shoulders and upper back for a better "shelf" during squats.

"Consistency over intensity. 2 minutes a day changes everything."
